ARIEA's Price Opinion and Confidence Score

ARIEA's Inherent Market Price = $293,332

ARIEA's Confidence Score = 99.6%

A.R.I.E.A.'s Price Opinion and Confidence Score is the overall market price estimate and degree of certainty for 201 Begonia Trail. To derive this estimate, A.R.I.E.A. considers all available data, market volatility, available inventory, and subdivision characteristics. The resulting inherent market price is the likely price the subject will change hands in a balanced arm's length transaction where the buyer is under no duress to buy nor the seller under duress to sell. Confidence scores are based out of 100% with higher values indicating higher confidence. Confidence scores of 80% and above are considered strong. Scores below 80% indicate a large degree of market volatility and should be used with caution.

Combination of Factors (COF) Comparable Sales Approach to Value

COF Weighted Avg. = $293,947

The COF Comparable Sales Approach is designed to valuate homes in the context of their functional utility. It is built on the premise that homes sold in recent months with the same combination of factors (bedroom count, bathroom count, garage space, etc.) as the subject have the same functional utility to potential buyers while homes with differing characteristics have less similarity. To derive the COF Weighted Average, the comparable sales are weighted based on their COF similarity to the subject with more similar sales carrying higher weight in the analysis.

Adjustment Based Comparable Sales Approach to Value

Adj. Weighted Avg. = $293,496

The Adjustment Based Comparable Sales Approach is built on the premise of Occam's Razor and employs the theory that comparable sales which require lower amounts of adjustment to their sales price equate to the subject's market price with a higher degree of certainty. To derive the Adjustment Weighted Average, the comparable sales are weighted based on their total adjustment percentage. Comparable sales with lower adjustment percentages are given higher weight in the analysis.

Median Comparable Sales Approach to Value

Comparable Sales Median = $293,947

The Median Comparable Sales Approach represents the price where 50% of recent sales fall below the value and 50% of sales fall above. To derive the median comparable sales value, all sales are weighted equally. Unlike, the simple average approach, the median value is less vulnerable to statistical outliners and better represents the home's market price. When combined with the simple average, it also provides insight into the overall market volatility of the neighborhood. Properties which have a similar median and simple average values enable higher degrees of certainty in the overall market analysis.

Square Footage Approach to Value

Square Footage = $291,325

The square footage approach employs a simple cost per square foot calculation to derive the subject's market price. It is based on the premise that homes within a neighborhood have similar overall quality of craftsmanship and functional utility. While a weaker approach to home valuation, it does provide a strong 'gut check' capability. Properties which have similar square footage valuations similar to the other approaches indicate a high degree of certainty in the overall market analysis.
